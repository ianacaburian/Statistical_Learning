Description of FleeceWeight data.


[SEX] 			:Sex of lamb (1:male, 2:female)

[BT ]			:Birth typ of lamb ( 1:single, 2:twin, 3:triplet, ...)

[SPINFINE] 		:Spine fineness score of the wool

[yWool] 		:Yearling wool production of lamb(kg)

[yearlingAGE] 		:Age of lamb at first recording(days)

[AdultAge]   		:Age of Lamb when adult (days)

[Dag]  			:Dag Score of animal(Integer score 1:5)

[CCov] 			:Cruch cover Score of animal(Integer score 1:5)

[BDWR] 			:Body Wrinkle Score of animal (Integer score 1:5)

[Pregnancy] 		:Pregnancy statuse of animal (0:non-pregnant, 1:single baring, 2:twin baring etc..)

[BCS] 			:Body Condition Score of animal (Double value 1:5)

[Weight]  		:Weight of sheep Of Sheep(kg)

[DryWt] 		:Pasture Dry Weight (kg/ha), a measure of food availability for lamb

[Rain] 			:Amount of rain fall (mm/m2)

[Temp] 			:Temperature (Celcius)

[AdultWool]		:Adult wool production (kg)
